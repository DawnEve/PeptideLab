Nucleic Acids

Molecular Weight Conversion
These conversions have been written by Agilent's Technical Support group to provide guidance when converting the molecular weight of ssRNA and ssDNA. Guidelines are also provided for approximating nucleic acid molecular weights and determining absorbance units to nucleic acid concentration conversion.

Factors Influencing the Tm of Nucleic Acid Hybrids
These guidelines have been written by Agilent's Technical Support group to provide guidance when working with Nucleic Acids. The factors addressed in this chart are ionic strength, base composition, destabilizing agents, mismatched base pairs, and duplex length.

Equations for Calculating Tm
These guidelines have been written by Agilent's Technical Support group to provide guidance when calculating Tm. Included in this pdf are equations for calculating Tm for DNA-DNA hybrid, DNA-RNA hybrid, RNA-RNA hybrid, and Oligonucleotide probes; determining Nucleic Acid concentration; SI Units chart; determining Nucleic Acid purity; and detecting contamination.

Nucleic Acid Sizes and Molecular Weights
These guidelines have been written by Agilent's Technical Support group to provide guidance when working with Nucleic Acids. This pdf contains spectral constants for Ribonucleotides; spectral constants for Deoxyribonucleotides; determining the molar mass and moles of ends of a double-stranded DNA fragment; moles of ends generated by a restriction digest; and sizes and molecular weights of various RNAs.

Conversions for Nucleic Acids and Proteins
These guidelines have been written by Agilent's Technical Support group to provide guidance for molar conversions for Nucleic Acids, Molar conversions for Protein, and Protein/DNA conversions.

Growth Areas and Numbers of HeLa Cells in Various Culture Vessels

Gel Electrophoresis[Agarose和PAGE胶对DNA的分辨率]
